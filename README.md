# Whole Exome Sequencing Analysis & Variant Annotation
This repo contains documentation of the workflow and codes -but not patient data- for analysing WES data. This workflow has been developed over 2 years of working with genetic data in intersection between clinical and research studies in two different laboratories (i.e projects) and accumulates hundreds of hours of testing and reading through documentaion of various tools. There are already well-established pipelines (e.g [nf-wes](https://nf-co.re/raredisease/2.0.0/)) for this type of analysis and there is no need to reinvent the wheel. Despite that, going through this material here shall provide some details or notes that are not often mentioned in tutorials. 

Please feel free to reach out in case of any questions regarding the code m.hussein@stud.uni-goettingen.de!

> [!NOTE]
